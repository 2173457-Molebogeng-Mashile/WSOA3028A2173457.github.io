Character Rationale.

The character I have created, Naledi Chiloane, is someone who loves everything about fashion. I wanted her to come from a very underprivileged background to make her career path unconventional for someone like her. The fact that she made it all the way to university only to drop out is something she did not take lightly. Majority of her family is poor, so failure was not an option for her. I didn't want her to come from a well-known area like Soweto because it makes her success that much sweeter. To come from literally nowhere and make a name for yourself is very inspiring.

I made her a fashion blogger because her job lets her travel the city and keep learning about fashion. She is very outgoing and is someone who gets along with everyone, this helps her build connections and further her career.
