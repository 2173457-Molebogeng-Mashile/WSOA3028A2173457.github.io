SA fashion week is going digital!

Like many other good things, this pandemic has taken away runway shows. This year, however, South Africa’s biggest annual fashion event, South African Fashion Week, is going to be held online. SAFW has always served as a central networking and trading hub for designers, boutiques and the South African fashion media. It gathers a wide range of African designers in Johannesburg and gives them an opportunity to show their work with an audience that can truly appreciate it.

This year we are going digital. With top African brands such as The Bam Collective, Mantsho and Judith Atelier lined up, you won't be disappointed. Although I do miss the beautiful chaos of the live shows, hosting it online means everyone gets a front row seat, so don't miss it.
